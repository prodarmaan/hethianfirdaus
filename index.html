Overview

Hethian Firdaus (Arabic: هذيان فردوس "delirious paradise") is an upcoming UW/Seattle-based band that aims to embody ambient, minimal atmospheres into a vortex of avant-garde transcendentalism. Aiming for a dark and textured post-rock/ambient sound, Hethian Firdaus draws inspiration from experimental elements, minimal aesthetics, natural environments, and existential ideologies to push the current boundaries of music and invite their listeners to ascend into another realm of sound.

Regarding the aesthetic for Hethian Firdaus (visual, audial, and atmospheric), the Aesthetic page contains visuals and songs that support the overall vision.

Member Qualifications

Hethian Firdaus is currently seeking two to three instrumentalists and one vocalist. Roles that are currently open are:

- Vocalist
- Keyboardist(s)
- Guitarist(s)
- Bassist

Members are desired to be multi-instrumentalists, in which roles can be switched and other experimental ideas can manifest via the introduction of more niche instruments/sounds.

If you are interested in becoming involved with Hethian Firdaus as a member or affiliate, please reach out to Armaan via Instagram (@prodarmaan) or email (prodarmaan@gmail.com).

Armaan is a UW/Seattle based music producer and multi-instrumentalist known for his ambient and ethereal sound: he is the founder of Hethian Firdaus and Greyworld Ambience, a creative collective pushing an ambient/experimental atmosphere. Armaan will fulfill the role as the primary drummer for Hethian Firdaus.
